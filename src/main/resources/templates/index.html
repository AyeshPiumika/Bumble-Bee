About Us


An online shopping ordering loan system for a website is a feature that allows customers to purchase products using a loan through the website. It involves integrating with a third-party lender that provides the loan to the customer.
The process typically involves the customer adding items to their cart, proceeding to checkout, and selecting the loan option as their payment method. The website then guides the customer through the loan application process, which involves providing personal and financial information required by the lender.
Once the loan is approved, the website receives the payment from the lender, and the order is processed and shipped. The customer then makes repayments on the loan according to the agreed terms with the lender.
Having an online shopping ordering loan system on a website can make it easier for customers to purchase products they may not be able to afford upfront. It also provides an additional financing option for customers who may not qualify for traditional loans.
